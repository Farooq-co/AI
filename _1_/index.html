✅ Travel Checklist

Essential steps for planning your trip

Pro Tips
  • Watch Videos First: Get inspired by travel content from your chosen platforms to discover hidden gems and popular attractions.
  • Check the Weather: Review local weather conditions to pack appropriately and plan indoor/outdoor activities accordingly.
  • Compare It: Browse nearby hotels, compare locations, and read reviews to find the perfect accommodation for your stay.
  • Plan Your Budget: Use the gathered information to plan your detailed itinerary and budget accordingly.
  • Book in Advance: Once you've decided, book your flights, hotels, and tours early for the best deals.
